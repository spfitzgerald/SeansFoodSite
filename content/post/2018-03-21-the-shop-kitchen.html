What we ordered

The “French Melt” menu description: “Sticky, but worth it. Grilled french toast sandwich, seared ham, cheddar, and topped with real maple syrup”

My take: Uhhh, yeah a freaking cheesy breakfast sandwich on FRENCH TOAST with syrup!?!? This was exactly as decadent and delicious as it sounds.

Cost: $12.

Taste: 9/10

French Melt

The TUGBOAT menu description: “Eggs Benedict with a grilled buttermilk biscuit topped with avocado, 2 poached eggs smoked tomato hollandaise and your choice of meat” (we got ours with Carnitas)

My take: So I just absolutely love benedicts. So I figured this would just be a really good benedict. I was right, but what took me by surprise was how good the carnitas were - they stole the show. I always love runny eggs & Hollandaise with hot sauce (they have Cholula), but the carnitas really took it all to the next level. Awesome.

Cost: $14.

Taste: 9/10
